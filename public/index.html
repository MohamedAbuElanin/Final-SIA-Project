About SIA

SIA (Self Intelligence Analyzer) is a platform that guides you to understand your personality traits, learning style, and professional potential. By combining personality analysis with curated learning resources, SIA empowers you to make informed decisions for your personal and professional growth.

Features

Personality Assessment

Interactive test to evaluate your traits and learning style.

Dynamic Dashboard

Visualize insights and key takeaways from your analysis.

Curated Resources

Access personalized courses, books, YouTube channels, and roadmap.

User Accounts

Secure login and profile management.

Admin Tools

Manage assessments, users, and reports (for administrators).
